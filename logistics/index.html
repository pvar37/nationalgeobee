Logistics

We're excited about bringing a national competition back to the United States. You'll find general information about tournament logistics here.

School

School bees will be held between January 15, 2026 and March 15, 2026. While we give schools the freedom to decide how they select a school champion, we strongly encourage bees to be held during regularly-held class periods to ensure that all students can participate.

County

County bees will be held on March 27, 2026. School champions will all be invited to participate in a county bee against other school champions.

National

Regional bee champions will all be invited to participate in the 2026 National Geography Bee tentatively on May 9, 2026.
