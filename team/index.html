Meet the team

Rahem Hamid is a co-founder of the National Geography Bee and a Massachusetts state finalist in the 2017 National Geographic Bee. He is a 2025 graduate of Harvard College, where he majored in history and was an executive at The Harvard Crimson. He lives in the Boston area. You can reach out at rahem@nationalgeobee.org.

Pranay Varada is a co-founder of the National Geography Bee and the 2017 National Geographic Bee champion. He is a 2025 graduate of Harvard College, where he majored in statistics and government and was president of the Harvard Open Data Project. He lives in Washington, DC. You can reach out at pranay@nationalgeobee.org.
